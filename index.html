Get Started Today

Have you ever had an idea for a program or app but didn't know where to start? Porcupine is your answer, providing a database filled with starter codes for various types of apps in multiple languages.

A Collaborative Effort

The database will be constantly expanding as most, if not all, of the skeleton codes provided will be from programmers and dreamers like you who want to help you get your feet off the ground.

Help someone out today, upload your skeleton code!

Why Porcupine?

  • Porcupine is entirely free and will always be free.
  • Every single skeleton code will come from users just like you, allowing you to learn from like-minded thinkers and doers.
  • No matter what, every user will be able to access every file in our database.
  • Powered by MongoDB Stitch.
